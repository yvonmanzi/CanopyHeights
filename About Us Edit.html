ABOUT US


The resort was established in 1996 by chairperson Waridi Elizabeth.


It was initially supposed to be a gaming spot for different people to able to compete and hold tournaments but there was a gap of accommodation that needed to be taken care of. Therefore, that is how the idea of Canopy Heights was born and made into a reality


Our resort has three branches in Nakuru and Naivasha while the main and biggest one is in Nyali, Mombasa Kenya.
